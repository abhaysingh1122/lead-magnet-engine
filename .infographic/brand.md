# Abhay Singh Infographic Brand Config

## Style: Neuro Neo Brutalism (Neumorphic Warm)

## Layout
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) — always
- Background: Warm cream/parchment (#F5EFE0 or #EDE8DF). Never white. Never gray.
- NO logo — do not include any company branding or logo
- NO URL or CTA badge — no domain names, no pill badges
- Headline zone: Large bold condensed black headline — key stat or insight, max 8 words
- Body zone: 2-3 lines of supporting context below the headline
- Concept visual: Flat geometric illustration or diagram

## Brutalist Design Rules
- All UI elements (cards, badges, frames) have thick black borders (3px solid)
- Shadows: Solid offset drop shadows — 4-5px offset, zero blur, pure black
- No gradients — flat color fills only
- No blurred effects — everything sharp and hard-edged
- Grid-based layout — structured, elements aligned

## Typography
- Headline: Very large, heavy condensed weight, near-black (#18140E), max 8 words
- Body: 2-3 short sentences, regular weight, smaller size, dark color
- Font style: Bold condensed sans-serif headlines (Syne-like), clean regular weight for body (DM Sans-like)

## Color Palette
- Background: #F5EFE0 (warm cream) or #EDE8DF (parchment)
- Text: #18140E (ink / near-black)
- Borders and shadows: #000000 (pure black)
- Accents (pick 1-2 per graphic): gold (#C8B560), sage green (#6A9E7F), clay red (#C47070), slate blue (#5E86A8), teal (#4E9E98), lavender (#8B7EC8)

## Illustration Style
- Flat geometric shapes with bold black outlines and flat accent color fills
- Hard offset shadow treatment on illustrated elements
- Diagrams, flow arrows, node graphs — all hard edges and thick outlines
- No stock photos. No realistic faces. No 3D. No soft/organic illustration.

## Content Pattern
1. One bold stat or claim as the headline
2. 2-3 lines of supporting context below
3. One flat geometric concept visual

## What to Avoid
- Any logo, wordmark, or company branding
- Any URL, domain name, CTA badge, or pill
- Soft/blurred shadows
- Gradients or glass-morphism
- Light gray backgrounds
- Stock photos or realistic imagery
- Hex color codes near label text in prompts (Gemini renders them as literal text)

## Audience
Agencies and B2B businesses $500K-$10M+ — agency owners, ops leads, founders who need systems that run themselves
